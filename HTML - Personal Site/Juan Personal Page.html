Juan Aja Aguinaco

Co-Founder Extraordinaire

Juan is an entrepreneur, Co-Founder of Shyft Network Inc., Co-Founder of BlockUnity, and coach to startups, boutique service firms, and entrepreneurs in business planning, strategy, operations and marketing.

Juan started his career in corporate law, where he advised clients on corporate governance, strategy and securities law. This experience gave Juan a unique set of proven analytical and strategic planning skills that help his clients and his own business, plan, innovate, operationalize, execute and face the challenges of disruption.

As a strong communication and marketing professional, Juan leverages the power of storytelling to transmit compelling messages. Juan is also an avid content creator and journalist; He has written and produced political analysis blogs, podcasts, and photographic portfolios through the lenses of his analogue Pentax K-1000.

Juan holds an MBA from the Schulich School of Business, and a Bachelor of Laws from the Universidad Iberoamericana.
